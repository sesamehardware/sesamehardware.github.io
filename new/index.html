Game Studio

Servers for development, launch, and updates.

Game studios need data center hardware that can facilitate a team's workflow across locations, and with varied applications. Whether it's coding or character modeling, launching games or providing updates, game studios need custom solutions that fit their own unique style of creation. Sesame hardware can be built for any scale, and working with Sesame's team of data center experts, you'll be able to ask any questions and plan for the future. Whether it's the latest smartphone SHMUP or desktop MMO, we have the solution.

  • Focus: Real-Time Processing Power
  • Turnaround: 30 Days
  • Cost: $10,000 - $20,000

Cloud Storage

Servers that are always available with massive storage.

Providing cloud storage at any scale has specific requirements. Whether at an in-house enterprise or consumer level, users need the ability to both securely store and access their data at any time. Those servers need fast access to the public Internet and have built-in redundancy and reliability that depends on the right hardware. Sesame's experts have worked on the world's largest providers of these services, and can help your company deliver the same promise at any scale.

  • Focus: Cloud Storage
  • Turnaround: 90 Days
  • Cost: $30,000 - $70,000

Energy Exploration

Sending, processing, and analyzing massive amounts of data.

Energy exploration involves collecting massive amounts of data about potential sites. They can be around the globe, from remote locations in the Gulf or on the Northern Plains where cloud Internet access can be unreliable. But the need for fast processing of that data doesn't change, and companies need to be able to upload that critical information for processing and analysis. Sesame hardware can deliver on these requirements without fail, and our experts can create the perfect solution to meet any specific needs.

  • Focus: On-Demand Processing and Storage
  • Turnaround: 120 Days
  • Cost: $80,000 - $100,000
