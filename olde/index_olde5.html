Remanufactured Hardware

Not just refurbished, but rebuilt by data center experts.

In this digital, global economy, businesses depend on server storage to operate. Now, with Sesame, organizations have more options when it comes to building their server infrastructure. You're no longer limited to buying proprietary server equipment. Sesame is making hardware open and flexible.

Why are there more server options now? Sesame procures top-of-the-line servers from global hyperscale companies and remanufactures them to provide companies like yours with more options when building your server infrastructure.

Let's explain by comparing servers to cars. Imagine a world with no used cars, where your only option is to buy new. A similar dynamic has existed, until now, in the server industry. Organizations have had no choice but to build their server infrastructure with top-end, brand-new equipment. But not all shoppers—or companies—need the latest and greatest. Many prefer a reliable and more
cost-effective option.

Now imagine a car owner buys a brand-new sports car every three years. The high-performance machine traded in was made to last far longer than three years and has plenty of quality miles and years remaining. Upon purchasing the car from the original owner, the dealer conducts a thorough inspection. The dealership certifies the car's quality and sells it to a new owner as pre-owned and certified. With the confidence of the pre-certification, the second owner can expect many quality years with their new-to-them vehicle.

The same holds true for remanufactured server hardware.

All Sesame servers undergo extensive testing before being certified as remanufactured. Companies can expect many productive years from remanufactured servers at a more affordable rate. These servers are proven, effective performers. Sesame gives this powerful equipment new life with new owners, a method that is ground-breaking in the industry.

Mission Critical Design

Top of the line hardware built for reliability.

Servers are typically customized to meet the unique needs of each company during deployment. This means that, until now, companies had to buy new servers at significant scale, or their needs had to be limited enough to meet their computing requirements through a limited number of uniform used servers. Sesame is breaking that mold. With Sesame, you can have unprecedented mission-critical server configurations at scale for an affordable rate.

All Sesame servers are state-the-art from global hyperscale companies—which means they're identical and ready to be customized to your needs. This enables you to purchase 10 to 10,000+ servers with the exact same configuration.

With Sesame servers, your storage needs are easier to implement, support, and swap out. This simplicity and reliability dramatically decreases ownership cost. The cost of supporting thousands of traditional servers with an array of versions and configurations is highly prohibitive for most companies. It's a different story with Sesame. If a drive fails in a Sesame server, you just swap it out. Fewer headaches and more cash to put toward your business is a winning solution.

The only way to buy customized servers at scale without the proprietary price-tag is Sesame. Sesame will configure as many identical servers as needed for a given workload. Does your company need 3,000 high performance computing (HPC) servers crunching numbers or processing transactions? Sesame can do that. Does your company need 1,000 near-line or cold data storage servers? Sesame can make it happen. Whatever your custom server needs are Sesame can do it.

All servers have been "burned in," or rigorously tested, for three years and kept in an ultra-clean and well-maintained environment. With an estimated two thirds to three quarters of their life remaining in these servers, Sesame's customers can expect many productive, reliable years. These servers are designed to be on the front lines of your data center deployment and are perfectly poised to excel in a new capacity.
